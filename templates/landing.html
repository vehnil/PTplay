our mission

At thera_pixel, we believe that physical therapy should be more than just repetitive movements—it should be engaging, motivating, and accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our mission is to transform the traditional rehabilitation experience by merging evidence-based physical therapy with the excitement of gaming.

We create interactive, pixel-art games designed specifically to help patients practice the form, pacing, and repetitions of their prescribed exercises. By turning therapy into a game, we make recovery not only more enjoyable but also more effective. Our platform encourages patients to stay consistent, motivated, and mindful of their progress, all while following proper techniques critical for healing.

Through data-driven insights and personalized feedback, therapists can track patient improvements in real time, adjust routines, and ensure that each individual stays on the right path to recovery. Whether you're rehabbing at home, in the clinic, or on the go, thera_pixel empowers you to take control of your healing journey—one pixel at a time. Because recovery shouldn't feel like a chore—it should feel like leveling up.
