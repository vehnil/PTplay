pizza pursuit: game for recovery

"strengthen your arms, one slice at a time"

game overview

Pizza Pursuit is a fun and engaging game designed to help you exercise your arms, wrists, and shoulders. Perfect for those with upper-extremity pain, upper back pain, and light spasticity.

In this game, you'll move your arms to catch falling pizzas while engaging in therapeutic movements. Enjoy the challenge as you level up your skills and improve your rehabilitation progress.

materials needed

  • Resistance band
  • 5lb barbell weight
