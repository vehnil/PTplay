practice studio overview

our practice studio is an interactive rehabilitation tool designed to help patients refine upper extremity movements through real-time motion tracking and feedback. by comparing a user’s movements with a curated dataset of ideal spatial coordinates, the practice studio offers personalized guidance to improve accuracy, range of motion, and form.

tailored for more mature patients or those requiring targeted physical therapy, it supports focused skill development while also serving as a practice space to prepare for the more dynamic challenges of the Pizza Game. This dual-purpose platform empowers users to build confidence, enhance motor skills, and track progress at their own pace.

materials needed

  • Resistance band (optional)
  • 5lb barbell weight

you can also practice movements without any weights or resistance! we recommend this for first-time users.


left bicep curl

the left bicep curl exercise in our practice studio focuses on strengthening the bicep and improving elbow flexion—key components for daily activities like lifting objects or reaching. bicep curls are a fundamental part of physical therapy, helping to rebuild muscle strength, enhance joint stability, and improve overall upper limb function.
